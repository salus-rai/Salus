Introduction

What is Privacy Responsible AI?

The Privacy tenet of Infosys Responsible AI toolkit facilitates redact the PII and any sensitive data in an organization by detecting the privacy entities and giving options to analyze, anonymize and encrypt using models like Tesseract, Easy OCR and Computer Vision. We analyze privacy in images, videos, and text by reading through above document types, processing & applying the redacting technics.

Why is Privacy Responsible AI Important?

Privacy in Responsible AI is crucial for several reasons:

  1. Ethical Considerations:

  • Respect for Individual Rights: Protecting individual privacy is a fundamental ethical principle.

  • Building Trust: Transparency and responsible data handling are essential for building trust between individuals and organizations.

  1. Legal Compliance:

  • Adherence to Regulations: Numerous data privacy regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA) mandate how personal data can be collected, used, and protected.

  • Avoiding Legal Risks: Non-compliance can lead to significant legal penalties and reputational damage.

  1. Preventing Bias and Discrimination:

  • Fairness and Equity: Biased data can lead to unfair and discriminatory outcomes.

  • Protecting Vulnerable Groups: Ensuring privacy is crucial for protecting marginalized groups who may be disproportionately affected by AI.

  1. Maintaining Competitive Advantage:

  • Customer Trust: Privacy breaches can erode customer trust, leading to loss of business.

  • Brand Reputation: A strong commitment to privacy can enhance brand reputation and customer loyalty.

  1. Innovation and Progress:

  • Ethical AI Development: Prioritizing privacy fosters innovation in AI while avoiding negative societal impacts.

  • Long-Term Sustainability: Responsible AI practices ensure the long-term viability of AI technologies.
